Doctoral Student at ETH Zurich Switzerland





I am a second year PhD student at Animal Genomics ETH Zurich Switzerland.

I am now responsible for setting up the pipelines for analyzing population level (hundreds to thousands) of whole genome livestock sequencing data. I mostly using Snakemake for workflow design and management.

In the other sides, I am interested in data science particularly reproducible big data analysis and interactive big data visualization.

I mostly used Python 3, R, shell scripting and JavaScript (lately).
